About Me

For most of my life, I wanted to become a professional artist. In 2020, I began attending college for an AAS in graphic design to develop my skills so I could pursue that goal. Unfortunately, I found it unfulfilling and dull; marketing, logo design, branding—none of it appealed to me. Although I never found enjoyment in graphic design, I'm glad I finished the degree because it led me to my true passion when I took a web development class in my last semester.

Using logic to style and build a website was satisfying in a way that graphic design had never been. It made me want to explore paths I had never considered for before, and in 2023 I started pursuing an AAS in computer programming. It was difficult at times, but I had never been so motivated to learn and overcome the challenges.

Currently, I'm searching for a full-time programming job tha will push the limits of my knowledge and provide unique challenges to help me further hone the skills I developed in school. My preferred language is Java, but I also have experience with Python, JavaScript, and C#.
